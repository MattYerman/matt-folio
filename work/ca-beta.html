CA Beta

CA Technologies

We approached CA with a simple idea: build your technology stack around a trials-driven, website experience.

Long-standing technology hurdles prevented a truly seamless and speedy flow, so we re-thought the customer experience within a small "walled-garden" Beta site, removing unecessary clicks and working directly with the product teams to ensure the fastest approach towards trial conversion. We crafted six user scenarios that encompassed a wide range of wanted capabilities, such as the ability to compare products, chat with sales in your customer portal, manage users within your trial and upgrade to the premium version of your product.

Role — Art Direction, Design, UX
